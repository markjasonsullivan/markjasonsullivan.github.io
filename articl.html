Articl

Title: UX Designer of Articl

Date: (4 Months) Senior at Penn State

Problem: Wanted to know what articles my intellectual friends were reading.

Solution: A platform that allowed you to share highlights of articles with your circle of friends.

Process: Step 1) Interviews Step 2) Requirements, Step 3) Personas, Step 4) MVP, Step 5) Sketches, Step 6) Mockups, Step 7) User Testing, Step 8) Design and Build "Current" 9) Launch to test assumptions 10) Pivot if necessary

Lessons: Build a simple MVP first to test assumptions and continuously test iteration after iteration.
